100 20 . Mason Currey <Daily Rituals : Women at Work> . , 4 . , . .

This project is based on research conducted on 100 female artists who were active during the modern and contemporary periods. From this research, the creative routines of 20 female artists were translated into music box scores. The investigation into the routines of these female artists drew inspiration from Mason Currey's book, Daily Rituals: Women at Work. Each of these female artists continued their work in their own unique ways, and their work routines could be broadly categorized into four types. Over an extended period, these work routines became a distinctive indicator and pattern representing each individual. Translating the created patterns into a piece of music is a hymn to the lives of female artists who persevered in their daily creative endeavors without giving up.
